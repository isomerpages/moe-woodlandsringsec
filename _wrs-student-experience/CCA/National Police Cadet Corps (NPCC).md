<h4><strong>About the CCA</strong></h4>
<p>Our mission in NPCC is to develop our cadets into community leaders who&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>value discipline and teamwork</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>face challenges boldly and pursue excellence</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>fight crime in partnership with Singapore Police Force</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>are caring and responsible</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Our cadets go through a <strong>structured</strong> and <strong>progressive</strong>  <strong>training</strong>  <strong>programme</strong> that
challenges and develops them into selfless and resilient leaders who possess
integrity and persevere in adverse and rugged contexts. Their growth and
development is guided by NPCC’s Learning Path:&nbsp;</p>

<p>Some of our unique programmes include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Leadership Programme - Cadets plan camps, online and face-to-face training
and execute them to learn experientially what it means to be a leader.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Outdoor activities such as campcraft training and adventure training camps
at NPCC HQ’s campsite on Pulau Ubin</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Courses related to work of Singapore Police Force such as Crime Scene
Investigation (CSI), Cybercrime Prevention, Police Procedures, Basic Law
and SG Secure</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Crime Outreach programmes such as Community Safety and Security Programme
and Police Youth Ambassador</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>0.22 revolver shooting</p>
</li>
</ul>

<h4><strong>CCA Details</strong></h4>
<p><strong>School Term</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Regular training every Friday, 2.30 pm – 5.30 pm (if any school or public
holiday falls on a Friday, the regular weekly training will be held on
Monday instead.)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Courses for Proficiency Badges (e.g. Police Knowledge, Civil Defence,
CSI)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Overall, there are about 1.5 days of training every week.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>School Holiday</strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>School Camps (Sec 1 Orientation, Sec 3 Leadership Camp, Annual Unit Camp)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sec 2 Adventure Training Camp at Pulau Ubin</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sec 2 Cadet Leader Course</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sec 3 Enhanced Crime Scene Investigation Course</p>
<p><strong><em>All these camps and courses are compulsory for promotion</em></strong>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Others</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>There will be certain compulsory learning journeys and activities scheduled
by NPCC HQ on weekdays after school and/or during holidays. <u>Cadets are expected to attend these activities</u>.
Parents / guardians will be informed via Parents Gateway in advance.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Expenses to be borne by students</strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Cadet Leaders T-shirt (partially subsidised by the school)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enrichment courses (partially subsidised by the school and the remaining
amount to be paid in cash or Edusave by the cadets)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Overseas expedition organised by HQ (paid by cadets in cash)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Cadets will need to bear the full cost for any lost / damaged uniform
part or accessories provided by HQ</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Targeted Student Profile</strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Students who like to be outdoors</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students who enjoy working as a team and would like to learn to lead</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students who have an open heart to learn from others</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students who have no serious medical condition (e.g. fractured limbs,
chronic asthma, severe allergic to grass)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students who value discipline and enjoy challenges</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>CCA Trial Procedures</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Complete a simple situational test</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Interview with the teacher officers</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Target number of students</strong>
