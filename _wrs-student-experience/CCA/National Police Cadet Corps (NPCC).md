<h3>About the CCA</h3>
</td>
<td style="width: 1.34078%; height: 481px;" valign="top">
<h3>:</h3>
</td>
<td style="width: 66.8156%; height: 481px;" valign="top">Our mission in NPCC is to develop our cadets into community leaders who
<ul>
 	<li>value discipline and teamwork</li>
 	<li>face challenges boldy and pursue excellence</li>
 	<li>fight crime in partnership with Singapore Police Force</li>
 	<li>are caring and responsible</li>
</ul>
Our cadets go through a structured and progressive training programme that challenges and develops them into selfless and resilient leaders who possess integrity and persevere in adverse and rugged contexts. Their growth and development is guided by NPCC’s Learning Path:

Some of our unique programmes include
<ul>
 	<li>Leadership Programme - Cadets plan camps, online and face-to-face training and<span style="font-weight: 400;">execute them to learn experientially what it means to be a leader.</span></li>
 	<li>Outdoor activities such as Campcraft and camps at NPCC HQ’s campsite on Pulau Ubin</li>
 	<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Courses related to work of Singapore Police Force such as Crime Scene Investigation (CSI), </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Cybercrime Prevention</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">, Police Procedures, Basic Law and SG Secure</span></li>
 	<li>Crime Outreach programme such as Community Safety and Security Programme and Police Youth Ambassador</li>
 	<li>0.22 revolver shooting</li>
</ul>
