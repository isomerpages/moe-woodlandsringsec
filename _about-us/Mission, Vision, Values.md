<h3><strong>School Motto </strong><br>Forward with Wisdom</h3>
<blockquote>
<p><sub>Our motto reflects our commitment to be forward-looking, move ahead with courage, discernment, and integrity. It symbolizes our belief that education is not merely about acquiring knowledge but about applying wisdom to navigate an ever-changing future.</sub>
</p>
</blockquote>
<h3><strong>School Vision</strong></h3>
<h4>Forward-looking, Compassionate, Wise and Resilient Leaders</h4>
<blockquote>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Forward-looking</strong> – curious, innovative, adaptable to change</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Compassionate</strong> – caring, inclusive, and ready to serve</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Wise</strong> – thoughtful, reflective, and principled in decision-making</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Resilient Leaders</strong> – persevering through challenges and
inspiring others with empathy</p>
</li>
</ul>
</blockquote>
<h3><strong>School Mission</strong><br>Empowering every student to grow and lead with purpose and values</h3>
<blockquote>
<p><sub>Our mission reminds us that education is a shared endeavour between staff, students, and the wider community. As a school of opportunities, we provide platforms for student agency and focus on nurturing each student’s potential, guiding them to be purposeful learners, and empowering them to lead and contribute meaningfully to society.</sub>
</p>
</blockquote>
