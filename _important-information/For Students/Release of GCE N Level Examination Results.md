### **Release of GCE N-Level Examination Results on 18 December 2025**

As per press release on Thu, 11 Dec, school candidates will be receiving their GCE N(A)-Level and N(T)-Level results on **Thu, 18 Dec from 2.00 pm.**

School candidates are to return to school from 1.30pm onwards and assemble in the school hall by 1.50 pm. Students are to be **properly attired** in their **school uniform**. Parents/guardians may accompany their child/ward to the school to collect their results.

School candidates who are unable to collect their results in person should inform the school/form teacher via email. They can appoint a proxy to collect a physical copy of their results on their behalf. In the email to the school/form teacher, please indicate the **full name** of the proxy.

Proxies are required to produce the relevant documents for the school's verification when collecting the results.
