<h4>Release of GCE O-Level Examination Results on 14 Jan 2026 </h4>
<p>As per press release on Thu, 7 Jan, school candidates will be receiving
their GCE O-Level results on <strong>Wed, 14 Jan from 2.00 pm</strong>.</p>
<p>School candidates are to return to school from 1.30pm onwards and assemble
in the school hall by 1.50 pm. Students are to be <strong>properly attired </strong>in
their <strong>school uniform</strong>. Parents/guardians may accompany their
child/ward to the school to collect their results.</p>
<p>School candidates who are unable to collect their results in person should
inform the school/form teacher via email. They can appoint a proxy to collect
a physical copy of their results on their behalf. In the email to the school/form
teacher, please indicate the <strong>full name</strong> of the proxy.</p>
<p>Proxies are required to produce the relevant documents for the school's
verification when collecting the results.</p>
